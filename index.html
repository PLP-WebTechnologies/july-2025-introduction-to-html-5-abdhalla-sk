Historical Heritage

Fort Jesus

This 16th century Portuguese fort is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and Mombasa's most iconic landmark.

Old Town

Wander through narrow streets lined with ornate Swahili architecture and bustling markets.

Pristine Beaches

  • Jomo Kenyatta Public Beach: Popular beach named after Kenya's first president
  • Nyali Beach: Favorite spot for both locals and tourists
  • Bamburi Beach: Excellent for water sports and family outings

Swahili Cuisine

Don't miss these local specialties:

  • Biryani - Fragrant spiced rice dish
  • Pilau - Aromatic rice with meat
  • Viazi Karai - Spicy fried potatoes
